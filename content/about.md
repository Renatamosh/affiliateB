Bridge Playbook is an independent guide to the best online bridge platforms in 2026.

## What We Do

We research, test, and review every major online bridge platform — Bridge Base Online (BBO), Funbridge, RealBridge, and others — so you can find the right platform without spending hours testing each one yourself.

Every review on this site is written after hands-on testing. Pricing is verified directly from each platform's billing page, with the verification date clearly marked.

## Our Approach

We follow three principles:

**Independence.** No platform pays us for coverage or placement. Reviews reflect our honest assessment.

**Verification.** Pricing, feature availability, and ACBL masterpoint eligibility change. We mark every piece of pricing information with the date we verified it.

**Clarity.** Online bridge platforms are not all the same. We tell you exactly who each platform is for — and who it isn't for.
