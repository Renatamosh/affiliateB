faq:
  - question: "How do I start playing bridge online for the first time?"
    answer: "The easiest way is to create a free account on Bridge Base Online (BBO) at bridgebase.com. Once registered, click 'Play Bridge' and select a 'Social' or 'Beginner' table. You can also play against robots for free, which is a good way to get comfortable with the interface before joining live games."
  - question: "How do I join an ACBL masterpoint game online?"
    answer: "Log into BBO and go to the ACBL Masterpoint Games section. Games run throughout the day at scheduled times. You need a BBO premium subscription ($5.99/month) and an active ACBL membership to earn official masterpoints. Register for your chosen game at least a few minutes before start time."
  - question: "How do I play bridge with friends online?"
    answer: "On BBO, click 'Play Bridge', then 'Friends Tables' to create or join a private game. You can invite specific players by their BBO username. RealBridge also works well for group play because it adds video, which makes online bridge feel more like sitting together at a real table."
  - question: "Can I play online bridge on my iPad or iPhone?"
    answer: "Yes. BBO, Funbridge, and RealBridge all have iOS apps. BBO and Funbridge also have Android apps. The BBO app is free to download. For the best tablet experience, Funbridge has the most polished mobile interface, though BBO's app works well for most purposes."
