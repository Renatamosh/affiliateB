faq:
  - question: "What is the best online bridge platform for intermediate players?"
    answer: "Bridge Base Online (BBO) is the most widely used platform for intermediate players who want live human opponents and access to ACBL masterpoint games. Funbridge is the better choice for players who prefer solo AI training with the strongest available AI engine. Most serious intermediate players use both."
  - question: "What bidding system should club players use online?"
    answer: "For most club players, Standard American (SAYC) or 2/1 Game Forcing are the best choices for online play because they are supported by all major platforms and the most common among the player pool. Acol players are well served on BBO and Funbridge. Whichever system you choose, consistency with your regular partner matters more than which system you pick."
  - question: "How do I improve at bridge using online platforms?"
    answer: "The fastest improvement comes from replaying your own deals and reviewing the hand records afterward. BBO's hand record feature and Funbridge's post-deal analysis tools both make this straightforward. Focus on borderline decisions — the hands where your judgment was tested — rather than the easy ones."
  - question: "Is Bridge Base Online free?"
    answer: "BBO has a genuinely complete free tier that includes live tables, the partnership desk, Vugraph kibitz, and robot games. The BBO+ subscription ($5.99/month) adds an ad-free experience, advanced robots, and ACBL masterpoint access for some games. The free tier is sufficient for social and casual play."
