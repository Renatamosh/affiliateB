faq:
  - question: "How much does it cost to play bridge online in 2026?"
    answer: "Bridge Base Online (BBO) costs $5.99 per month or $59.99 per year for a premium account, which is required for masterpoint games. Funbridge costs $15.99 per month with annual discounts available. RealBridge is priced per session starting around $4–6 for club events. Trickster Cards is free. All prices are in USD and verified quarterly."
  - question: "Can you earn ACBL masterpoints playing online?"
    answer: "Yes. ACBL masterpoint games run daily on BBO and require a premium BBO subscription and an active ACBL membership. You earn silver points (online masterpoints) from most games, with special events offering gold and platinum points. Many club directors also run online club games through BBO that award club masterpoints."
  - question: "What is the best online bridge community for experienced players?"
    answer: "BBO has the largest active community with over 100,000 players online at peak times, making it the best place for competitive players to find quality opponents at any hour. Funbridge has a strong European community and good club features. RealBridge is preferred for social groups and clubs that want video connection."
  - question: "Is online bridge as good as playing in person?"
    answer: "For many experienced players, online bridge is now their primary game — especially since the pandemic accelerated adoption. The card play itself is identical. What you lose online is the social atmosphere, the physical feel of the cards, and live partner communication. What you gain is the ability to play anytime, find strong opponents, and access your hand records for review."
