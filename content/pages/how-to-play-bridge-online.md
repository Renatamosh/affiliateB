faq:
  - question: "How do I start playing bridge online for the first time?"
    answer: "Go to bridgebase.com and create a free BBO account. Once logged in, click 'Play Bridge' and choose a Social or Practice table. You can play against robots for free, or join a table with other players. A free account gives you access to most features, though masterpoint games require a premium subscription."
  - question: "Do I need to know bridge before playing online?"
    answer: "You need to understand the basic rules of contract bridge before playing in live online games. If you are learning from scratch, start with the Funbridge tutorial mode or BBO's practice robot tables, which are more forgiving. Playing in live games before you know the basics will be frustrating for you and your partners."
  - question: "Is online bridge the same as playing in person?"
    answer: "The card game itself is identical — same rules, same bidding, same scoring. What changes is the social experience: no physical cards, no in-person partner communication, and no table atmosphere. Video platforms like RealBridge partially restore the social element by adding live video. Most experienced players find the adjustment easy within a few sessions."
  - question: "Can I play online bridge with my regular partner?"
    answer: "Yes. On BBO, you can invite a specific player to be your partner using their BBO username. On RealBridge, you join the same club event together. Funbridge allows you to create private rooms for partner practice. Most platforms also support private club events where the same group plays together regularly."
