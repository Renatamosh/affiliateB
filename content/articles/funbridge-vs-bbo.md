**Quick Answer.** BBO and Funbridge solve different problems and most serious bridge players in 2026 use both. BBO ($5.99/mo BBO+ on top of a complete free tier) is the live human community and the only sensible choice for ACBL masterpoint earners. Funbridge ($15.99/mo Premium) is the strongest solo AI training platform — the Argine engine and replay-the-same-deals format make improvement measurable. If you must pick one: BBO if you play with humans for masterpoints; Funbridge if you train solo against AI.

## Bridge Base Online (BBO)

**Price (verified May 2026):** Free tier with full access · BBO+ at $5.99/month (30-day free trial) · Robot tournament entries ~$2 per event via BB$.

BBO is the world's largest online bridge platform — approximately 11.6 million monthly visits and the default home for online ACBL-sanctioned games. The free tier is genuinely complete: live tables, the partnership desk, Vugraph kibitz, and tournament entry. BBO+ adds an ad-free experience, the advanced robots, and the digital Bridgerama+ magazine.

**Best for:** Live human play, ACBL-sanctioned tournaments, online masterpoints, intermediate-to-advanced players who want the largest community in online bridge.

**Watch out for:** The interface is functional but dated by 2026 standards. Mobile apps are improving but still feel secondary to the web client.

## Funbridge

**Price (verified May 2026):** Free tier with 2 deals/day · Premium $15.99/month or $159.99/year · Premium+ $24.99/month or $249.99/year · 1-month Premium free trial · Deal Packs $5.49 for 50 / $22.99 for 400.

Funbridge is the strongest solo AI bridge trainer in 2026 — the proprietary Argine engine and approximately 170,000 active users. The signature feature is the daily competitive deal system: every Premium subscriber plays the same hands against the same robots, with scores compared across the platform.

**Best for:** Solo improvement, intermediate-to-advanced AI training, mobile play in 20-minute sessions.

**Watch out for:** Funbridge does not award ACBL masterpoints on standard play (the eBridge Cup partnership with BBO is the single exception). Acol support is functional but secondary to SAYC; 2/1 system support is limited. Premium+ at $24.99/month is expensive without Bridgerama+ readership.

## Our Recommendation

**For most intermediate and advanced players**, the right setup is to use both. BBO ($5.99/month BBO+) for live human play and ACBL masterpoints; Funbridge ($13.33/month on the annual plan) for solo improvement against the strongest AI in online bridge. The combined cost is under $20/month for a serious training setup.

**If you must pick one:**

- Pick **BBO** if you play with humans, want ACBL masterpoints, or want a complete free tier you can use indefinitely.
- Pick **Funbridge** if you want to improve against AI in 20-minute sessions on your phone, and ACBL masterpoints aren't a priority.
