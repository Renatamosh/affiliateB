If you are new to online bridge, you will almost certainly encounter these two names first: Bridge Base Online (BBO) and Funbridge. They are the two most popular platforms for English-speaking players, and they take very different approaches to the online bridge experience.

## Bridge Base Online (BBO)

**Price:** Completely free (premium cosmetics available)

BBO is the world's largest online bridge platform. At any hour of the day you will find thousands of live tables — robots, casual games, club events, and major tournaments. The community is enormous and the breadth of play styles available is unmatched.

**Best for:** Players who want to find a live game quickly, tournament players, those who want a completely free experience.

**Watch out for:** The interface is functional but dated. New players can find it overwhelming at first. Stick with the "robot game" section while you find your feet.

## Funbridge

**Price:** Free basic tier / from £9.99/month for full access

Funbridge is the most beautifully designed bridge app available. Its AI opponents are widely considered the strongest on the market, and the structured daily deals — where you play the same hand as thousands of other subscribers and see how you compare — are genuinely addictive.

**Best for:** Solo practice, beginners, players who want guidance and structure, anyone who values a clean modern interface.

**Watch out for:** The free tier is quite limited. Meaningful use requires the subscription, and the live player community is smaller than BBO's.

## Our Recommendation

For **complete beginners**, start with Funbridge. The clean interface, excellent tutorials, and patient AI opponents create a welcoming learning environment.

For **regular players** who want a live community and tournaments, BBO is unbeatable — especially since it is completely free.

Many serious bridge players use **both**: Funbridge for daily solo practice and improvement, BBO for live competitive play.
