Online bridge has come a long way in recent years. What was once a niche experience reserved for tournament players has become a rich, welcoming world that any bridge enthusiast can step into. Here are five reasons 2026 is the perfect time to start.

## 1. The Platforms Are Better Than Ever

Bridge Base Online, Funbridge, and RealBridge have all invested heavily in their software over the past two years. The interfaces are cleaner, the AI opponents are stronger, and the onboarding experience for beginners is genuinely excellent. Funbridge in particular has launched a guided learning path that takes you from your very first deal to confident online play.

## 2. Play Any Time, Anywhere

The great constraint of traditional bridge — needing four people in the same room at the same time — no longer applies. Log on at 2am if you wish, and you will find a game on BBO within seconds. AI robot games mean you can practise completely alone whenever the mood strikes.

## 3. Video Bridge Feels Like the Real Thing

RealBridge has shown that online bridge does not have to feel impersonal. With built-in video and audio for all four players, a RealBridge session feels genuinely like sitting at the same table. Many clubs that moved online during 2020 have stayed with RealBridge permanently because their members prefer it.

## 4. Your Game Will Improve Faster

Online play offers something that club bridge cannot: instant review. Every platform records your hand history. Funbridge's analysis tool shows you the computer-optimal line of play after every session. If you want to improve, there has never been a better environment for it.

## 5. The Community Is Welcoming

The online bridge community — particularly for players over 60 — is warm, patient, and inclusive. Beginners are welcomed, questions are answered generously, and the culture is one of encouragement. Bridge Playbook's own community forum is full of players who remember being new to the game.
