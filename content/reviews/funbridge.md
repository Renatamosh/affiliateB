Funbridge, published by Goto Games, is the premium solo training platform for bridge in 2026 — approximately 170,000 active users, the proprietary Argine bidding engine, and a unique replay-the-same-deals format that lets you compare your score against the rest of the platform.

Originally French (it remains dominant in France), Funbridge is now genuinely international and particularly strong among intermediate-to-advanced improvers who want structured training without partner pressure.

**Important caveat for North American players:** standard Funbridge play does not award ACBL masterpoints. The eBridge Cup (an annual partnership with BBO) is the single exception.

## Getting Started on Funbridge

Download the Funbridge app from the App Store or Google Play, or access it through a web browser. Registration is free and takes one minute. The app opens with an onboarding flow that surfaces the daily competitive deals — this is the format you'll use most. New subscribers get a 1-month free trial of Premium.

## The Interface: Best-in-Class Mobile-First Design

Funbridge's interface is the most modern and intuitive of any major bridge platform. Cards are large and clearly rendered. The bidding box is easy to use on both desktop and touchscreen. The game flow — bidding, play, then analysis — is smooth and logical. The mobile apps are designed mobile-first rather than as ports of a desktop client, which shows in the polish.

## Argine: The Strongest AI in Online Bridge 2026

Funbridge's AI engine — Argine — is the strongest commercial bridge AI as of 2026. Bidding is consistent intermediate-to-advanced, regularly updated by the Funbridge team, and designed to make the kinds of errors that human players make so practice transfers to real play. SAYC is the default bidding system; Acol is supported but secondary in polish. 2/1 system support is limited compared to BBO.

## The Replay Format: Why It Works for Improvement

Funbridge's signature feature is the daily competitive deal system. Each day, new deals are published — every Premium subscriber plays the same hands against the same robots, and scores are compared across all players worldwide. This comparative element makes improvement measurable in a way that no human-partnered platform can match.

## ACBL Masterpoints and Funbridge — The Honest Answer

Funbridge does not award ACBL masterpoints on standard play. The single exception is the eBridge Cup, an annual partnership event with BBO. If masterpoint earning is your primary motivation, Funbridge is not the right primary platform — use BBO or RealBridge. If you're playing for skill development, the absence of masterpoints is irrelevant.

## Pricing: Free vs Premium vs Premium+

The free tier offers 2 deals per day and basic AI play. Premium at $15.99/month (or $159.99/year) unlocks unlimited deals, ad-free play, and full match formats. Premium+ at $24.99/month adds the digital Bridgerama+ magazine and advanced post-game analysis. Deal Packs ($5.49 for 50 deals, $22.99 for 400) are an alternative for players who don't want a recurring subscription.
